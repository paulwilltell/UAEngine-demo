The Insight

Punctuation is emotional data

"???" vs "?"

Triple questions = frustration spike. Single question = curiosity. Same character, different meaning.

"!!!" vs "!"

Multiple exclamations = enthusiasm burst (positive). Combined with negative words = rage.

"WHY" in caps = frustration. "LOVE" in caps = enthusiasm. Same style, opposite emotions.

"..." Trailing
